Tacos

Simple ground beef tacos, serves up to ten delicous tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 10 taco shells
  • 1/2 head shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef until brown
  2. Stir salsa into beef
  3. Heat taco shells
  4. Serve taco shells with beef, salsa, lettuce, and cheese
