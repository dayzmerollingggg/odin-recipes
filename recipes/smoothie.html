Watermelon Smoothie

Cool down in the summer, or winter with a blend of frozen Watermelon, strawberries, and yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen watermelon cubes
  • 1/2 cup plain vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy
