Vanilla Ice Cream

Your traditional, homemade vanilla ice cream, in just 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and stir until sugar dissolves
  2. Fill tub with ice cream mixture
  3. Freeze
